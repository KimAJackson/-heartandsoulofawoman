About the Book

Enter a heartfelt journey through poems reflecting love, identity, hope, healing, perseverance, and womanhood. Kim A. Jackson shares words that inspire and connect deeply with readers.

Meet Kim A. Jackson

Kim A. Jackson was born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri and developed a lifelong love of literature and poetry from an early age. Influenced by Maya Angelou, Langston Hughes, Shel Silverstein and others, she cultivated a voice rooted in emotion, experience, and reflection. The Heart and Soul of a Woman fulfills a dream decades in the making.
